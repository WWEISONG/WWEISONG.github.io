Wei Song

I am current a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at UNSW Sydney, working with Scientia Professor Jingling Xue. I received my PhD in Computer Science from UNSW Sydney, supervised by Scientia Professor Jingling Xue.

My research interests lie at developing defenses against adversarial threats, provenance watermarking frameworks for AI-generated content, and secure biometric authentication methods, with applications spanning AI systems.
