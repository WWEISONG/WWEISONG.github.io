Wei Song

I am current a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at UNSW, working with Prof. Jingling Xue, Prof. Yulei Sui, Prof. Zhenchang Xing, and Prof. Liming Zhu. I received my PhD in Computer Science from UNSW Sydney, supervised by Prof. Jingling Xue.

My research interests focus on AI Security, with particular emphasis on the following areas:

AI watermarking for images, videos, and code Adversarial video attacks and defenses Security analysis for Video Large Language Models Fairness for Video Large Language Models AI CAPTCHA
