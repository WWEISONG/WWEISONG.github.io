Wei Song

Postdoctoral Research Fellow, UNSW Sydney

I am a postdoctoral researcher working on the security, reliability, and real-world deployment of AI-enabled systems. My research focuses on AI systems security, trustworthy media, adversarial robustness, and multimodal model safety, especially for video intelligence and large language model ecosystems.

I collaborate with academic and industry partners to build practical technologies for content authenticity and model trustworthiness. Current research focus on security and safety challenges in AI-generated media, robust multimodal reasoning, and secure AI-powered infrastructure for critical domains such as energy systems.
